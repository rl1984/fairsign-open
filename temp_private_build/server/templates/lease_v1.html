6. Use of Premises

The Premises shall be used exclusively for residential purposes. The Tenant shall not use the Premises for any illegal or commercial purposes without prior written consent from the Landlord.

7. Maintenance and Repairs

The Tenant agrees to maintain the Premises in good condition and to promptly notify the Landlord of any necessary repairs. The Landlord shall be responsible for major repairs and maintenance of structural elements, plumbing, and electrical systems.

8. Utilities

The Tenant shall be responsible for payment of all utilities including but not limited to electricity, gas, water, internet, and cable services, unless otherwise specified in writing.

9. Rules and Regulations

The Tenant agrees to comply with the following:

  • No smoking inside the Premises
  • Quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM
  • No unauthorized pets without prior written approval
  • No alterations to the Premises without Landlord consent
  • Compliance with all applicable laws and building regulations
10. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing 30 days written notice prior to the end of any rental period. Early termination by the Tenant may result in forfeiture of the security deposit.
